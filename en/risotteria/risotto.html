Rice with and

Recipe for 4 people: 400g of Carnaroli rice

Finely chop an onion. In a saucepan, fry the chopped onion with a few butter for about 5 minutes.

Stir in rice, fry it until translucent over a high heat and keep stirring with a wooden spoon. Add wine and stir till absorbed completely. Add a ladleful of hot stock, keep stirring and adding stock until absorbed. When the rice is almost cooked...

When the rice is cooked, turn the heat off and serve.
